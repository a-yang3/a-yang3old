About Me

I believe good experiences lie in the intersection of understanding people from a neurological perspective, crafting designs that feel good, and bringing ideas to life with prototypes and programming. I'd love to learn more about UI/UX and Product Design as I navigate the rest of my college years.

In my free time, catch me making music with the UC Berkeley Symphony Orchestra, eating my way through the bay's spiciest Sichuan restaurants, or hiking around the best state ever.
